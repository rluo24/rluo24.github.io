Description:

Air Emory is a student-led air quality sensor network. The first of its kind at Emory that displays real-time data around campus. This promotes awareness, general knowledge, and engagement for students and faculty alike about our interaction with the environment.



I facilitate the front-end development of the Air Emory website to upload various sensor systems, update data readings, and am working to improve the framework/user experience.



Air Emory also participates in community initiatives such as Science ATL and the Georgia Air Quality Challenge. We are collaborating with local schools to set up many more air quality sensors from Airly to monitor air quality in underrepresented areas in the near future.
